Undertale

  • Made in September 2015
  • Made by Toby Fox
  • Some sprites done by Temmie but mostly done by Toby
  • 3 Main Ending's, 1 Secret Ending
  • 3 Endings being named Neutral, Pacifist, and Genocide
  • Neutral ending has alot of different endings only changing dialogue.
  • Pacifist and Genocide you can only strictly play the game one way. If you mess up, you're automatically set up for a Neutral ending
  • Is/Was very popular
  • Had at most 6 Hours of Gameplay to Completion
  • Genocide is the fastest, Pacifist is slowest, Neutral is in between.

Deltarune

  • Made in October 2018
  • Made by Toby Fox
  • Some sprites done by Temmie but mostly done by Toby
  • Unfinished 2 out of 7 possible chapters are completed.
  • Claimed by Toby Fox that this is not connected to the Undertale universe at all. (But who knows how true that is.)
  • Made after Undertale
  • Has some characters from Undertale in this game too.
  • Chapter 1 has 2 different endings with barley any differences.
  • Chapter 2 has 3 different endings. 2 with barley any differences. 1 very different.
  • 3 Endings in Chapter 2 be named Neutral, Pacifist, and Snowgrave
  • Chapter 1 takes about 3 hours to complete. Chapter 2 takes about 6-7 hours to complete.
